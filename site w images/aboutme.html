About Me

I've spent the past 10+ years working across different areas of music

These days my time is spent researching, studying, coding, and sleeping.

A brief history

×

I have always had an interest in having fun. Enjoying
myself at all times has been my obsession for as long as I
can remember. Being creative and feeling free is usually a
cheatcode for enjoying myself. When I was very young, I would
draw cartoon characters and give them voices. I made short comic strips,
and was constantly trying to make people around me laugh.


A little later, after my Mom tried (and soon put down) the guitar, I decided I would
try it out. This was a moment that would eventually lead me to Berklee
College of Music for guitar. I had private instruction under Tomo
Fujita (James Valentine, John Mayer), and gained so much perspective
of perspective and life experience. While there I made lifelong
friendships.


My interest in technology and problem-solving has been
a constant in my life. I've an avid video-game player and, as with
all my hobbies, I need to know *everything* about it. Basically
programming, to me, is an artform and I am so
excited to continue my journey.
